Project Octoblu

Workspace Automation

With the market for smart devices growing exponentially, IoT is becoming an increasingly critical component of enterprise IT strategies. In conjunction with the Citrix enterprise suite, Project Octoblu offers a powerful solution for automating, monitoring, and analyzing the enterprise workspace.

With Octoblu, Citrix is creating the Future of Work, where context-aware environments synthesize data from a variety of sources to respond to the needs of the user. Imagine a personalized desktop launching when you approach a workstation, your next GoToMeeting starting when you enter a conference room, your documents automatically saving to ShareFile. With Octoblu, this is already possible.

Enterprises can also use Octoblu to automate essential tasks for a variety of systems, from HVAC and lighting to wireless networks and security. IT administrators can create automation workflows for system monitoring, notifications, logging, and debugging processes.

Beyond automation, enterprises can leverage Octoblu to collect and analyze valuable data about customers, employees, office buildings, and more. Octoblu can be integrated with existing big data analysis tools like Splunk for a robust and seamless solution.
