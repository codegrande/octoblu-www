Octoblu and the Enterprise

With the market for smart devices growing exponentially, IoT is becoming an increasingly critical component of enterprise IT strategies. In conjunction with the Citrix enterprise suite, Octoblu offers a powerful solution for automating, monitoring, and analyzing the connected enterprise workspace.

With Octoblu, Citrix is pioneering the Future of Work, where context-aware environments synthesize data from a variety of sources to respond to the needs of the user.

Workplace Automation

  • Launch a personalized desktop when you approach a shared workstation
  • Start your next GoToMeeting when you enter a conference room
  • Automatically save your documents to ShareFile
  • Auto-connect to nearby printers via iBeacons

System Automation

  • Automate Workflow approvals for XenApp StoreFront
  • Collect, analyze, and respond to data from customers, employees, and office buildings
  • Automate system monitoring, notifications, logging, and debugging
  • Integrate with big-data analysis tools like Splunk and TempoIQ
  • Adjust the office lights or air-conditioning to reduce energy costs
