Our Platform

Octoblu enables companies to create IoT services with secure real-time exchange of data. The services are built on an open communications and management platform that supports a variety of protocols for physical devices to communicate seamlessly with each other, people, legacy applications, and cloud services. Through public, private, or hybrid clouds users can connect, design, process, and analyze the flow of information. All services have been designed through a robust security and right management architecture.

Meshblu

Octoblu’s Meshblu platform is the core communication layer of our offering. It is a secure, scalable cloud-based system enabling communication between smart devices, sensors, cloud resources, Arduinos, Raspberry Pi’s, and any other IP based hardware device, non-IP based hardware device or software API.

Gateblu

Gateblu is the Octoblu gateway offering and is the smart software hub working within the Octoblu platform connecting to Meshblu any smart device that has an IP address and any not-so-smart devices lacking an IP address. This software based hub is supported on Mac, Linux or Windows operating systems.

Mobiblu

Mobiblu is the Android or IOS based mobile hub application enabling a powerful tool that controls most of the functions of Octoblu from your mobile device allowing the connection of plugins, devices (including BLE) or sensors to the app on the mobile device and then allows the triggering of a flow with the click of a button. Mobiblu brings the power of the Octoblu and Meshblu platform’s to your fingertips.

Microblu

Microblu is Octoblu’s Device Operating System that allows the connection of devices such as Arduino’s, Spark’s, Beagle bone, Intel Galileo, Pinoccio, Raspberry Pi or Tessel’s to Meshblu with or without the use of a CPU. Microblu is powerful enough to spin up a Meshblu cloud directly on the deployed device creating even more possibilities for advanced control.
